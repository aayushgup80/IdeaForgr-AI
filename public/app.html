💡 Ideas & Tabs

Click ＋ New Idea to open a separate workspace for each startup concept. Each idea gets its own set of chat tabs: Chat, Research, Pitch, and Notes by default, but you can add unlimited custom tabs with the ＋ button in the tab bar. Double-click any idea tab or sub-tab to rename it. Hover over a tab to reveal its × delete button. The AI is cross-tab aware — when you chat in the Pitch tab, it already knows what's in your Research tab.

⚒ Chat & Voice Input

Type in the input box and press Enter to send (Shift+Enter for a new line). The AI knows your idea name and the last 6 messages of conversation, so answers are always in context. Click the 🎙 microphone button to use voice input — it transcribes your speech in real time and auto-sends when you stop speaking. Requires Chrome or Edge. Use the ＋ dropdown to activate a mode (Research, Pitch, SWOT, Names) which pre-fills the input with a smart prompt you can extend before sending.

✨ Smart Start & Auto-Naming

Every new idea greets you with 5 starter prompt chips covering the most important startup questions — business model, target customer, competitors, MVP scope, and problem definition. Click any chip to instantly send that question. After your first message in a new idea, the AI will suggest a name based on your description — accept it with one click or dismiss it. The empty state disappears permanently once an idea has its first message.

🔬 Workspace Tools

The right panel has 6 one-click tools. Research delivers a structured market report using live Google Search grounding (falls back to AI training data if search quota is exceeded). Summary builds a concise investor-ready elevator pitch. Names generates 10 creative, brandable startup names. Pitch writes a 60-second investor pitch script. SWOT runs a full Strengths/Weaknesses/Opportunities/Threats analysis. Logo opens the SVG logo generator. Every result opens in a popup with Copy and ↓ Download buttons, and is also saved to the history list below the tools. Click any saved entry to reopen it, or × to delete it. Saved results persist across page refreshes.

🔄 Iterative Refinement

After any workspace tool generates a result, a row of Refine chips appears at the bottom of the popup. These let you iterate on the result without starting over — for example, after a Pitch you can click "Make it punchier" or "Add specific numbers" and the AI will rewrite just that part using the original result as context. Each workspace tool has its own set of refinement options tailored to what makes sense for that type of output.

✨ Logo Generator

Click Logo in the workspace panel or ✨ Generate Logo in the + dropdown. Choose a primary color, a style, and an optional tagline. Eight styles are available: Minimal/Lettermark, Badge/Shield, Geometric, Tech/Circuit, Wordmark (full name), Neon Glow, Monogram (stacked letters), Split Color (diagonal two-tone). The result is a pure SVG — right-click the preview image to save it, or expand "View SVG code" to copy the raw markup into any design tool.

⚡ Compare Ideas

Click ⚡ Compare in the action bar to open the comparison tool. Select any two ideas from the dropdowns and click Compare. The AI reads all the notes, chats, and research from both workspaces and produces a side-by-side analysis covering Market Potential, Competitive Advantage, Key Risks, a score out of 10 for each, and a Final Verdict. The comparison uses live Google Search grounding when available. Use the ↓ Download button inside the panel to save the result as a text file.

📄 Export to PDF

Click ↓ Export PDF in the action bar to download the current idea as a dark-themed, multi-page PDF. The document includes a cover page with your idea name, a section for each tab (Chat, Research, Pitch, Notes, and any custom tabs you've added) with messages formatted as a conversation, and an appendix of all saved workspace results. Page numbers are added automatically. Requires pdfkit installed on the server (npm install pdfkit).

🗑 Data & Storage

All ideas, chat history, and saved results are stored in your browser's localStorage and persist across page refreshes automatically. Click 🗑 Clear Data in the action bar to wipe everything and start fresh (this cannot be undone). Individual saved workspace results can be deleted with their × button. The idea counter in the top-right nav shows how many ideas you currently have.
